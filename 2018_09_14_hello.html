[En] Hello everyone

Posted on pią 14 wrzesień 2018 in Misc

DISCLAIMER I'm not native English speaker so if you find any errors be more than a welcome to send me a message about it.

Hi everyone! My name is Mikołaj Słupiński. I am pursuing a master's degree both in Mathematics and Computer Science at University of Wrocław. My main interests right now are machine learning and statistics. My non science-related hobbies include gym and male fashion.

I decided to start a technical blog, in order to better understand some more complicated topics. To be honest, I really doubt that I can cover valuable topics better than other online resources. I treat this blog merely as a new way to self improvement, not only in my computer science understanding and but also in English usage.

I will publish in English as well as in Polish, because in my opinion there is not enough widely available Polish materials covering the most complicated topics.

If you ever find any errors (especially grammatical ones) don't hesitate to mail me. I don't have a proofreader, so all help would be appreciated.

I hope you will find materials posted here valuable and I wish you pleasant reading.
